ABOUT US

At Fixed Gear Bikes, we live and breathe the simplicity and style of fixed-gear riding. Our passion is helping riders—from beginners to seasoned urban cyclists—get the most out of their bikes. Whether it’s custom builds, precision tuning, or routine maintenance, we combine expert craftsmanship with a love for the cycling community. We believe in the beauty of minimalism, the thrill of the ride, and the connection between rider and machine. Every gear we adjust, every frame we touch, and every wheel we spin is a step toward the perfect ride. Stop by, share your story, and let’s keep your fixed-gear rolling smoothly on every street.
